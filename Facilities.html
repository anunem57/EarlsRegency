FACILITIES


The Earl’s Regency is a getaway to the hills of royalty and grandeur not only because of its location, but due to the
facilities it offers as a luxury Kandy hotel in Sri Lanka. We combine the mystique of our natural atmosphere with the
lavishness of our services to ensure your dream vacation becomes a reality.


Our on-site facilities range from lounging in our splendid swimming pool to an assortment of sports such as badminton. Guests
are urged to make full use of our gym or head on over to our Ayu Spa, where a soothing massage awaits. Golf enthusiasts
will appreciate our close proximity to the Victoria Golf and Country Resort, to which we can arrange reservations and transport.


Our on-location shopping arcade has many souvenirs and trinkets for purchase, while our reception desk will be happy to assist
with day-to-day services such as currency exchange, postal service, and the arrangement of taxis and other transport. For those who
like to mix business and pleasure, our business and conference facilities are some of the best in the region. We have a dedicated team
who can cater to your every request, allowing you to connect with the outside world through any platform.

SWIMMING POOL

After a long day of exploring the cultural wonders of the city, cool off with a nice dip in the swimming pool at our luxury hotel in Kandy.

Following the contours of the natural rock formation it’s built into, the swimming pool overlooks a breathtaking view of Kandy and is surrounded by a relaxing landscape. We’ve got the sunbeds, umbrellas, and towels covered – just bring your swimsuit and you are good to go.

The pool is open from 7 am to 10 pm and our poolside Dip ‘n’ Dive Bar is open from 8 am to 8 pm daily with snacks and cold drinks. A separate children’s pool is available, and though there are lifeguards on duty, we ask that parents supervise their children at all times.

CHILDREN AND FAMILIES

What’s a family vacation, well, without family? The Earl’s Regency is a prime choice when it comes to family hotels in Sri Lanka – having services and amenities solely dedicated to the “little people” in our lives.

More Details

ACCOMMODATION

When making your reservation, put in a request for one of our interconnecting rooms. These are limited and may not always be available, but an extra bed or cot can be accommodated for your comfort.

DINING

Our restaurant has a separate children’s menu created to satisfy even the pickiest eater. Complimentary highchairs are also available in the restaurants.

LEISURE

A separate children’s pool is located near the hotel’s main swimming pool. Shallow and child-friendly, your kids will make their own poolside memories. There is also a children’s playground on hotel grounds complete with swings, slides and a jungle gym.

CHILDREN’S MENUS

Please ask your waiter or restaurant manager for the separate children’s menu.

CHILDREN’S POOL

There is a small circular children’s pool next to the main swimming pool. Please supervise your children when they are playing here.

PLAYGROUND

We have a free children’s playground with swings, slide and climbing frame. Please supervise your children when they are using the playground.



About Us

Earl's Regency Hotel is a 5-star hotel located in Kandy, Sri Lanka. It is a luxurious hotel that offers spacious rooms, a spa, an outdoor pool, and a variety of dining options. The hotel is also home to a number of meeting and event spaces.
