WEDDINGS AND FUNCTIONS




Sri Lanka, in its entirety, is filled with wondrous customs and rituals for any event, but Sri Lankan weddings are by far one of the most celebratory and festive occasions on the island. Traditional Kandyan weddings are filled with detailed perfectionisms. Known for its beautiful attire, from the white saree for the bride and the Mul Anduma for the groom, so many elements tie together a Sri Lankan wedding. Let Earl’s Regency give you a wedding fit for Kandyan royalty on your special day.
